### Local SEO Focus Areas
1. **Google Business Profile optimization** — Category: "Tutoring Service" / "Coaching Center," with EAMCET, JEE, and Intermediate listed as services, geo-tagged photos, and weekly posts around exam dates.
2. **Localized landing pages** — Separate pages for "EAMCET Coaching in Vijayawada," "EAMCET Coaching near Benz Circle," "EAMCET Coaching near Patamata/Labbipet" — competitors are listing-heavy but not page-heavy, leaving room to win on dedicated content.
3. **NAP consistency** — Same Name, Address, Phone across Justdial, Sulekha, Google, and the website footer.
4. **Local backlinks** — Listings/partnerships with Vijayawada schools, junior colleges, and local education bloggers/YouTube channels.
5. **Review generation strategy** — Active collection of Google reviews mentioning "EAMCET Vijayawada," "Intermediate tuition Vijayawada" naturally in review responses.
6. **Schema markup** — LocalBusiness + Course schema for rich snippets in search results.
